About NeoPlace

NeoPlace was created to guide students through the often confusing and overwhelming placement journey. Many graduates jump between YouTube videos, random websites, seniors' notes, and mixed advice—yet still feel unsure about where to begin or how to stay consistent. This leads to frustration, inconsistency, and a lack of direction. NeoPlace solves this by giving students a clear, structured, and supportive path from start to finish.

Structured Learning, Simplified

Courses Module

The Courses module acts like a guided mini-curriculum for placements. Students get neatly organized content covering technical fundamentals, communication skills, interview strategies, and placement-focused topics—helping them learn confidently at their own pace.

Practice That Builds Real Skill

Coding Practice Section

The Coding Practice section offers beginner-to-advanced DSA problems. It works like a personal coding playground—helping students practice consistently, track progress, and build strong problem-solving skills without feeling overwhelmed.
